✦ Keep Them Looking Perfect

Aftercare Tips

🚿

Keep them dry for 24 hours

Avoid steam, swimming, and sweating for the first day after your appointment.

🧴

Avoid oil-based products

Oils break down lash adhesive. Use oil-free micellar water to cleanse around your eyes.

🪮

Brush daily

Use a clean spoolie every morning to keep lashes neat and separated.

😴

Sleep on your back

Reduces friction and pressure on lashes, extending the life of your set.

✋

Don't pick or pull

Let lashes shed naturally with your natural lash cycle to protect your naturals.

✦ Common Questions

FAQ

How long do lash extensions last?
Lash extensions last the full cycle of your natural lashes — usually 2–3 weeks before infills are needed. With good aftercare and infills every 2–3 weeks, you can maintain your set indefinitely.
What's the difference between Korean Lash Lift and LVL?
Both lift your natural lashes from the root — no extensions involved. The Korean Lash Lift uses a more intense curl rod for a dramatic, eye-opening effect. LVL (Length, Volume & Lift) adds more volume and a softer, longer-lasting curl. Louise can recommend the best option for your lash type.
How do I prepare for my appointment?
Arrive with clean lashes — no mascara, lash serum, or eye makeup. Remove contact lenses before a lift or tint. Avoid caffeine before your appointment as it can cause eye fluttering. That's it!
Can I wear mascara with extensions?
We don't recommend it — extensions already give a mascara effect. If you do, only use a water-based, oil-free mascara on the tips only, and never a waterproof formula as it's very hard to remove without damaging the lashes.
