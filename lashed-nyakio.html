Got questions?

We've Got Answers

How long do lash extensions last?
+

With proper care, lash extensions typically last 3–4 weeks. Natural lash shedding means you'll start to see gaps after 2–3 weeks, which is why we recommend infills every 2–3 weeks to keep your set looking full and fresh.

Can I wear mascara with lash extensions?
+

We recommend skipping mascara entirely — your extensions already give you that full look! If you must use it, only apply to the tips and use a water-based (not waterproof) mascara. Waterproof mascara weakens the adhesive bond and shortens the life of your lashes.

How do I care for my lashes after the appointment?
+

For the first 24–48 hours: avoid water, steam, and oil-based products near your eyes. After that, gently brush your lashes daily with a spoolie, avoid rubbing your eyes, and sleep on a silk pillowcase if possible. We'll send you a full aftercare guide after your appointment!

Is waxing painful?
+

There's a brief moment of discomfort, but we use a gentle formula specifically designed to minimise pain while effectively removing hair from the root. Most clients find it very manageable — and it gets easier with each session as the hair grows back finer.

How long before an event should I book?
+

We recommend booking lash extensions 2–3 days before your event — this gives the adhesive time to fully cure and any initial irritation (rare) to settle. For waxing, 3–5 days before is ideal to allow any redness to fade so your skin looks its absolute best.
