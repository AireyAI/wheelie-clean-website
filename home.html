About Wheelie Clean

Add your team or van photo here

We're Wheelie Clean — a local, family-run bin cleaning business proudly serving Cumbria. We started because we believed everyone deserves a clean, hygienic bin without the hassle. Using eco-friendly, biodegradable cleaning products, we power-wash and sanitise your wheelie bins right on your doorstep.
