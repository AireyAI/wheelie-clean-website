Built for the Creative World

ARTERIA was born from a simple frustration: finding a professional recording studio, a skilled videographer, or a unique event space shouldn't require hours of searching across dozens of disconnected platforms.

Frequently asked

Does ARTERIA take a commission per booking? ▾
Yes — ARTERIA charges a 10% service fee on each booking, paid by the guest. Hosts receive 100% of their listed price. This keeps hosting plans affordable while funding platform improvements.
Can I switch plans at any time? ▾
Yes. You can upgrade or downgrade at any point. Upgrades take effect immediately; downgrades take effect at the end of your current billing cycle. No penalties, no lock-in.
What counts as an "active listing"? ▾
Any listing that is published and visible to guests counts as active. Listings you've paused or archived do not count toward your limit. You can have unlimited draft listings on any plan.
Is there a free trial on Creator Pro? ▾
Yes — Creator Pro comes with a 14-day free trial, no card required. At the end of the trial you'll be asked to add a payment method to continue. If you don't, your account reverts to Explorer automatically.
Do guests pay anything to use ARTERIA? ▾
Browsing, saving, and messaging on ARTERIA is always free for guests. The only charge is the 10% service fee added at checkout on each booking — transparent, and shown in the price breakdown before you pay.
